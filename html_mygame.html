How Many Hours Have You Been Alive?: The Guessing Game

Have you ever wondered how many hours you have been alive? Ever wanted to guess and see if you were right?

If so, then this game is the answer for you! Whether you are 5 or 500 (vampires/lycans/immortals of all kinds), this game provides a sort of, mildy entertaining way to guess and guess and guess and guess until you're so frustrated that you pull out a calculator to find the exact answer! Fun right?!

How to play

  • Step 1
    • Enter your age and guess in the spaces provided below, and click the "Submit" button.
  • Step 2 (3 Parts)
    • (Part 1) If you're guess is right on the money:
      • You've won the game and probably have a calcultor in your hand or have a super computer-esque brain.
    • (Part 2) If you guess too high:
      • You will be notified your guess is too high and will graciously have the opportunity to try again.
    • (Part 3) If you guess too low:
      • You will be notified your guess is too low and again, graciously be allowed to guess again.
  • Step 3...not an actual step but something important to note!
    • You have an infinite number of guesses, no aid is coming. So unless you have the aformentioned super computer-esque brain or are in fact an immortal with the free time to guess until correct, I reccomend using a good old fashioned calulator, I prefer Texas Instruments, to figure out the correct answer.
